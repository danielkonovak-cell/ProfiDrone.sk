Detailed Overview of Technologies and Services

The DJI Agras T50 is a state-of-the-art tool designed to transform agricultural operations into a precise, data-driven process with minimal waste and maximum yield.

  • Dual Atomization Spraying System – extremely fine and uniform droplets.
  • Droplet size: 50–500 µm, Flow rate: 16–24 L/min.
  • VRA & RTK: precise dosing according to map (Variable Rate Application), centimeter-level navigation accuracy.
  • Orchard Mode, Obstacle Sensing, Terrain Following: full automation and safety.
  • Efficiency: up to 21 ha/h, batteries charged in 9–12 minutes.
